Our Ocean Freight Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored to your needs

FCL Shipping

Full Container Load services for exclusive use of entire containers. Ideal for larger shipments with direct routing and reduced handling.

  • 20ft, 40ft, and High Cube options
  • Door-to-door delivery available
  • Fixed schedules and transit times

LCL Shipping

Less than Container Load services for smaller shipments that share container space. Cost-effective solution with flexible volumes.

  • Pay only for the space you need
  • Consolidated shipping options
  • Regular departures on all routes

Special Cargo

Specialized solutions for oversized, heavy, or high-value cargo requiring custom handling and equipment.

  • Flat rack and open top containers
  • Project cargo expertise
  • Hazardous goods handling

Trade Lane Management

  • Emerging market coverage
